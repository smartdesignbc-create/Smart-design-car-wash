# Smart Design Car Wash
### Premium Car Wash Management System

> **Professional · Simple · Powerful**

---

## Overview

Smart Design Car Wash is a complete management platform for car wash businesses. Dark premium theme with cyan and blue accents. Manage daily operations, staff, customers, inventory, equipment, and finances — all from one clean dashboard.

---

## Features

- **Dashboard** — Revenue, vehicles done, staff on duty, weekly chart, stock alerts
- **Daily Operations** — Vehicle log, status updates, package tracking
- **Staff Management** — Clock in/out, profiles, positions
- **Customer Management** — Customer database, visit history, preferred packages
- **Inventory & Stock** — Low stock alerts, category filters, reorder tracking
- **Equipment** — Service schedules, condition tracking, maintenance alerts
- **Daily Financials** — Cash/card/EFT revenue, expenses, net profit, save daily records
- **Reports** — PDF and Excel export, package performance
- **Settings** — Business info, users, notifications, share panel
- **Share Demo Button** — Generate expiring client demo links (24h / 48h / 7d / 14d)
